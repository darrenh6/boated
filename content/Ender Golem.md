The **Ender Golem** has three different attacks, which are a standard melee attack (10 – 14 damage), an Earthquake attack (10 – 16 damage), and a Void Rune attack (10 – 14 damage).

Although the Ender Golem is the miniboss of the Ruined Citadel, it drops the more valuable item: [[Cataclysm Items#Void Core|Void Cores]].
